Questions

Is Tracks really free?

Yes — completely free, with no account, no trial, and no paywall. Every feature is available to everyone.

Do I need to download or install anything?

No. Tracks runs entirely in your browser using the Web Audio API. Open the page and start making music.

Where are my projects stored?

On your own device. Tracks never uploads your music. Your work stays local in your browser, and you can save a project file or export audio whenever you want.

Can I use it on a phone or tablet?

It works best on a desktop or laptop, but the studio is responsive and usable on tablets and phones for sketching ideas.

Can I import samples or use a MIDI keyboard?

Yes — drag in audio files, load SoundFont (.sf2) instruments, import MIDI files, and play or record with a connected MIDI keyboard.
